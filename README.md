a macropad! Yet to get the stuff from hackclub as the form is down.

## shell:
- made of abs plastic, constructed with 2 parts, the base and the lid and 4x m3 screws

## PCB:
- designed in kicad
- 2 layer pcb
<img width="873" height="809" alt="PCB drawing 1" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ab6d6cc5-20f1-4f60-9473-959219838c20" />

## parts list:
- 1x Seeed XIAO RP2040
- 9x through-hole 1N4148 Diodes
- 9x MX-Style switches
- 1x EC11 Rotary encoders
- 1x 0.91 inch OLED display (the pin order is GND-VCC-SCL-SDA, MAKE SURE YOUR PCB MATCHES)
- 9x white blank DSA keycaps
- 4x M3x16mm screws
